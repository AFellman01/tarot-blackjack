Tarot Blackjack

It's tarot blackjack!
There are four cards below. By clicking on a card you will draw from that suit and add its value to your score.
You win the game by hitting 21. You lose if you go over 21.
The value of each card is hidden from you until you draw it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will reshuffle your cards.
